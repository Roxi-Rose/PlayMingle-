Tic Tac Toe

X's Turn

Objective

To correctly guess the number randomly generated by the computer within 5 attempts.

How to play

  • Select a number within the range of 0 to 100
  • if guess is not correct retry if there are remaining attempts else you loose

Tips for the User

  • Start with an initial guess in the middle of the range (e.g., 50) and adjust up or down based on the feedback provided by the game.
  • Pay attention to the remaining attempts to manage your guesses effectively.
